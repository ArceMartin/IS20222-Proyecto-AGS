What is cosine similarity?
Cosine similarity measures the similarity of two vectors. 
It measures the cosine of the angle between two vectors projected in a multi-dimensional space. 
We can use this metric to determine how similar two documents are irrespective of their size.

cos(a,b) = a . b / ||a|| ||b||
